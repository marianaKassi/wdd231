Website Site Plan

ArtVision Ivory Coast

Site name

ArtVision Ivory Coast

This name represents a platform showcasing contemporary Ivorian art and artists. "ArtVision" evokes artistic vision, while "Ivory Coast" specifies the geographical and cultural context.

Optional available domain: artvision-iv.com

Site Purpose

ArtVision Ivory Coast aims to:

  • Present a virtual gallery of contemporary Ivorian artists
  • Facilitate discovery and purchase of local artworks
  • Provide information about artistic events in Ivory Coast
  • Offer a platform for emerging artists

Scenarios

Target audience questions:

  1. Who are the most promising contemporary Ivorian artists?
  2. How can I purchase an artwork featured on the site?
  3. What are the upcoming art events in Yamoussoukro
