Site Plan: Ethnic Promotion in Côte d'Ivoire

Celebrating Cultural Diversity and Unity

Site Name

IvoireCulture Connect

This name combines "Ivoire" (French for Ivory, representing Côte d'Ivoire) with "Culture Connect" to emphasize the connection between different ethnic groups through cultural understanding.

Optional domain availability: ivoireculture.org

Site Purpose

The website serves as an educational platform and cultural hub that:

  • Showcases the rich diversity of Ivorian ethnic groups and their traditions
  • Promotes cultural understanding and national unity
  • Provides resources about ethnic heritage preservation
  • Features cultural events, traditional practices, and educational materials
  • Connects communities through shared cultural experiences

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Student Researcher

"I'm a university student researching Ivorian ethnic groups for my anthropology class. Where can I find reliable information about the cultural practices and social structures of major ethnic groups like the Akan, Krou, and Mandé peoples?"

Scenario 2: Cultural Event Organizer

"Our organization wants to promote ethnic harmony through cultural festivals. How can we learn about traditional ceremonies and connect with cultural representatives from different ethnic communities in Côte d'Ivoire?"

Scenario 3: Diaspora Member

"As an Ivorian living abroad, I want my children to learn about our cultural heritage. What resources are available to teach them about our ethnic traditions, languages, and history?"
