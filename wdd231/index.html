About Me

My name is Mariana Kassi from Ivory Coast. I grew up in an environment rich in culture and tradition, which has shaped my personality and my passions. I'm passionate about community development, education and technology. I am actively involved in projects aimed at improving the lives of young people in my region. Apart from my social commitment, I love exploring the natural beauty of my country and sharing its wonders with others. Music and dance are also an integral part of my life, allowing me to celebrate our traditions and connect with people.
